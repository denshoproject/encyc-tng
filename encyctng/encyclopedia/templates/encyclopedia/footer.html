This Is A Live Project

The Encyclopedia, featuring over over 1,700 articles, is an evolving project that explores key concepts, people, events and organizations involved in the forced removal and incarceration of Japanese Americans during World War II.

Written by diverse contributors and enriched with photos, documents, and videos from Densho's digital archives and other sources, it is continually updated with new articles and features.
